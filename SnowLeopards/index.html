Snow Leopards

Snow Leopards are amazing animals.

As an adult, they average around 72 lbs.

Their scientific name is Panthera Uncia

They are carnivours, which means that they eat other animals.

They can reach up to 2.5 - 4.9 ft. excluding their tail.

Their gestation period is 101 days.

Their jump height is around 50 feet.
